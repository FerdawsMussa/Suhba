About this week

    This week invites you to explore how faith shapes daily living: embracing what is halal, finding balance between spiritual and worldly life, and following the example of the Prophet ﷺ. Through mindful learning and reflection, you will see lifestyle not as a set of rules, but as a path that nurtures purpose, character, and harmony in every aspect of life.
