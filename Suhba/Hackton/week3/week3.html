Week 3: Fasting

About this week

    This week invites you to explore the meaning of fasting: what it truly is, the spiritual purpose it serves, and what preserves or breaks it. Through mindful learning and reflection, you will discover fasting not as mere abstinence, but as a discipline of the heart—shaping intention, patience, and inner clarity.

1. What is fasting?

    Understand what fasting truly means in Islam, beyond abstaining from food, as an act of conscious worship.

2. Spritual goal of fasting

    Reflect on the spiritual goal of fasting to cultivate sincerity, self-discipline, and closeness to Allah.

    Learn

3. Fasting invalidators

    Learn what invalidates fasting in order to protect your worship and observe it with awareness and care.
