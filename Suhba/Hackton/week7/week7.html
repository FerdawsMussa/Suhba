Week 7: Tranquility

About this week

    This week invites you to reflect on the pursuit of tranquility in Islam: how inner peace, mindfulness, and reliance on Allah cultivate balance and serenity in daily life. Through thoughtful learning and reflection, you will see tranquility not as mere calmness, but as a conscious state of heart and mind that nurtures resilience, purpose, and spiritual well-being.

1. Spritual Calm

    Cultivate spiritual calm to deepen connection with Allah and find serenity in faith.
