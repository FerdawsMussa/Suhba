# Suhba

## Overview
Suhba is a social-impact technology company building digital spaces rooted in companionship, care, and belonging for the global Muslim community. Our platforms are designed to support learning, emotional well-being, and spiritual growth, helping individuals feel confident, connected, and never alone in their journey of faith.

---

## Mission
To ensure no one walks the path of faith alone. Suhba bridges the gap between curiosity and belonging, offering guidance, mentorship, and community connections for anyone seeking to grow in Islam.

---

## Core Values
- **Ihsan (Excellence in Design):** Islamic tools should be as intuitive, functional, and beautiful as the world’s best tech platforms.  
- **Sakina (Emotional Well-being):** Faith is a personal journey; we prioritize mental health and peer support in every feature.  
- **Amanah (Trust & Safety):** We operate with privacy-first principles and rigorous moderation to ensure a safe environment for all users.  
- **Radical Inclusivity:** Every seeker is welcomed, regardless of background or experience.  
- **Integrity (Sidq):** Providing verified, authentic, and balanced Islamic knowledge.  
- **Unity:** Strengthening bonds between new and established members of the Ummah.

---

## Suhba Ecosystem
- **Learning Lab:** Adaptive, gamified curriculums to support spiritual growth.  
- **Mentor Network:** A global, vetted network of empathetic mentors for guidance and support.  
- **Local Link:** Connecting users to local mosques.

---

## Features
- Structured learning pathways and interactive lessons  
- Secure mentorship requests and communication  
- Progress tracking and personal growth dashboards  
- Anonymous question submission with verified responses  
- Privacy controls to ensure safety and trust  

---

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** New Muslims (reverts) seeking guidance, learning, and companionship  
- **Secondary:** Mentors and educators  
- **Tertiary:** Curious seekers exploring Islam  
- **Institutions:** Mosques
